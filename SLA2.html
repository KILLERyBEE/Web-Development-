Types of Delicious Food

  • Pizza

    Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a round, flat base of yeasted wheat-based dough, usually with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then traditionally baked at high temperatures in a wood-fired oven. Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world for a variety of reasons. It is relatively cheap and easy to make, it is versatile, convenient, and can be customized to suit different tastes . Pizza is also a social food and comfort food that evokes happy memories for many people.

  • Vada Pav

    Vada pav is originated as an affordable street food in Mumbai, it is now served in food stalls and restaurants across India. It is also called Bombay burger in keeping with its origins and its resemblance in physical form to a burger. A plate of vada pav with seasoning of red chilli powder and a green chilli.Vada pav is a vegetarian fast food dish native to the state of Maharashtra. The dish consists of a deep fried potato dumpling placed inside a bread bun (pav) sliced almost in half through the middle. It is generally accompanied with one or more chutneys and a green chilli pepper.

  • Burger

    A classic burger typically includes a ground meat patty (beef, chicken, or veggie) served in a sliced bun with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments. Burger is a patty of ground beef grilled and placed between two halves of a bun . Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor.
