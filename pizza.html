History of Pizza

Pizza has a rich history that dates back to ancient times. It originated in Naples, Italy, and has since evolved into a global phenomenon. Immigrants brought pizza to the United States in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, leading to its widespread popularity.

Fun Fact

The world's largest pizza was made in 2012 in Rome, Italy. It measured 1.13 kilometers in diameter!
