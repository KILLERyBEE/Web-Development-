Types of Burgers

Welcome to the world of delicious burgers! Here are some popular types:

  • Classic Burger

    A timeless favorite with a juicy beef patty, fresh lettuce, ripe tomato, crispy onion, and a special sauce that adds the perfect flavor combination.

  • Veggie Burger

    For our vegetarian friends, the Veggie Burger features a plant-based patty made from a blend of vegetables and grains, topped with fresh veggies and a delightful veggie spread.

  • Spicy Chicken Burger

    Spice up your taste buds with the Spicy Chicken Burger! It boasts a crispy chicken patty seasoned to perfection and topped with spicy mayo for an extra kick.

  • BBQ Bacon Burger

    Savor the rich flavors of the BBQ Bacon Burger, complete with a succulent beef patty, crispy bacon, melted cheese, and a generous slathering of tangy barbecue sauce.
